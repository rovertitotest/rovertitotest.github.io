Mission

  • Characterize the Moon’s regolith, through the analysis of seismological data and infrared spectrometry.
  • To develop an open source framework of a modular rover which works as baseline for future projects.
  • To establish the antecedent of an fully autonomous moon rover capable of communicate with other rovers conforming a constellation.
  • To establish the antecedent of a low cost and open source rover capable of carrying out its mission after the moon night.
  • Use Deep Learning algorithms in order to predict future moonquakes based on a free access dataset.
